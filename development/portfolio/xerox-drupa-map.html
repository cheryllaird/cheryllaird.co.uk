Drupa is one of the world's largest trade fairs for the printing and media industry. Due to its high profile status within the industry, Xerox wanted to make most of the opportunity by creating an interactive map of the exhibition layout, to engage their customers prior to and during the event.

The map not only wanted to highlight the location of the Xerox stand at Drupa but also provide a brief overview of what can visitors can expect to find when visiting the stand.

With this project, there was a specific requirement that it was developed to be supported by mobile devices. This was to ensure that users could access and use it as a reference at the event itself.
