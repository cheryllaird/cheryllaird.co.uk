This particular project was a re-design of an existing site that was in place. The main reason for the re-design was to re-think the UX of the website; customers found the old website un-usable and confusing, especially during the quoting stage.

With that in mind I played a key role in the design of this website and undertook various planning stages which focused purely on the UX and flow of the quoting process. The end result was a much simpler and cleaner quote form, and has resulted in a higher number of policies being taken out online.
