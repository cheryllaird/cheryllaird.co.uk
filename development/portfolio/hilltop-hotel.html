The Hilltop Hotel is a family-run, traditional pub and steakhouse. The sites main objective is to drive business forward and provide useful information to the public such as the menus, opening times and special events which are available at the pub and steakhouse.

As a new website, everything from the initial design to the content and development was required by the customer.
