This personal project was inspired when trying to search for the perfect gift for my new-born niece. Whilst searching, I found a few variations of the 'Day you were born' certificates, none of which grabbed my attention in terms of design and authenticity. So I ran with this idea and put my own design onto the print.

The final product now is proudly being displayed in my niece's bedroom and since, I have produced further prints for the new additions in the family.
